## High Confidence

1. Question: Foundation Models vary across different aspects of data and use cases. Name two aspects.
   Answer: Foundation Models vary across Modalities and The Data Corpus

2. Question: There are different ways you can use GenAI Foundation models. Buy (Prompt only), Boost and Build. What is the main advantage of boosting?
   Answer: Enhances the ability of Generative AI model outcome providing client specific knowledge

3. Question: How are GenAI models different from traditional AI models?
   Answer: Traditional AI models are task specific and GenAI models can address wide range of tasks

4. Question: For a Utility client, the Development team completed migrating the Billing application to Dev environment in Google Cloud. After fixing all the Peer Review and Unit Test Case defects, code is promoted to QA environment for end-to-end testing. The testing team detected and reported around 105 defects. The Development lead wants to quickly understand the kind and pattern of defects, so that she can devise a strategy to prevent defects in next Iteration. She wants to leverage GenAI for this purpose.
   Answer: Classification and Summarization

5. Question: There are four ways we can enable Foundation Models for usage in TDLC. Which of the following is not a valid way?
   Answer: Consolidate multiple Foundation Models into one for specific use case

6. Question: As a GenAI Delivery Practitioner you will be implementing GenAI use cases for your client. As part of this implementation, you will write or customize Prompts. Which of the following NOT right about Prompt?
   Answer: Algorithm to execute specific Machine Learning tasks in the Technology Delivery

7. Question: Why is it crucial to strike a balance in prompt engineering on how much context to be provided in the prompt?
   Answer: To provide enough context for understanding without unnecessary details to get right output and reducing token cost

8. Question: You are executing Real Time "Root Cause Analysis of Job failure" use case leveraging Generative AI. There can be different root causes for different type of job failures. While writing Prompt we have to give example. Which of the following Prompt Engineering practice you will follow?
   Answer: Few-Shot Learning

9. Question: While writing Prompt for GenAI use case, you encounter a situation where the model consistently provides inaccurate responses. What should you do FIRST to address this issue?
   Answer: Review the existing prompts and refine them to provide clearer instructions

10. Question: For a Retail client, the users face multiple issues in their eCom portal. In these cases, users call Service Desk managed by Accenture.
